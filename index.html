The advantage of laser technology in surface treatment

With the development of new technology and new process, there are many traditional methods are not applicable. To some complex shaped molds, laser is an ideal surface treatment. Laser technology has no distortion, higher surface hardness, more wear resistant and long service life.

Using a laser: Lifetime up by 150%

Laser treatment of forming dies, such as are used in the manufacture of automobile parts, makes them more robust and resistant to wear, an industrial R&D partnership has found. Using a laser to alloy surfaces with filler material can increase the service life of dies by 150%.

Using Laser Marking to Improve the Traceability of Automotive Parts! 121 years ago, since the first car in the world “Benz” was invented, automotive industry is rapidly prospering. As the cars’ speed improved, the security issues receive more attention.

The main advantage of the laser marking on auto parts is quick, programmable and non-contact, and not affected by the required steps in the process of production and bad environment.

In the 1960s, “National traffic and motor vehicle safety act”, the first law about recalling car, was instituted in US. It makes strict requirements traceability of auto parts. Then the development of marking automotive parts technology started.

Now common methods of auto parts marking are: die casting, electric corrosion marking, stickers, screen printing, pneumatic and laser marking. Although the laser marking technology was the latest, but it got attention for its eternal marking.

Oil Pipe

Laser Cutting Technology is the typical application of slotted pipes laser cutting in petrochemical industry . When drilling petroleum, the key part of oil production is loosening bed and sand-proof. Ordinarily, numbers of tiny gaps around the pipe are processed to prevent sand . Laser cutting can achieve the special shape of kerf , such as wide outside and narrow inside, to make slotted pipe more efficient.
